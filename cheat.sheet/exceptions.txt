" =========================================================
" JAVA EXCEPTION HIERARCHY — CHEAT SHEET (TREE VIEW)
" =========================================================
"
" Object
"  └── Throwable
"       ├── Error                         " serious JVM problems (DO NOT catch)
"       │    ├── VirtualMachineError
"       │    │    ├── OutOfMemoryError
"       │    │    └── StackOverflowError
"       │    ├── LinkageError
"       │    │    ├── NoClassDefFoundError
"       │    │    └── ClassFormatError
"       │    └── AssertionError
"       │
"       └── Exception                     " recoverable conditions
"            ├── RuntimeException         " unchecked exceptions
"            │    ├── NullPointerException
"            │    ├── IndexOutOfBoundsException
"            │    │    ├── ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException
"            │    │    └── StringIndexOutOfBoundsException
"            │    ├── IllegalArgumentException
"            │    │    ├── NumberFormatException
"            │    │    └── IllegalStateException
"            │    ├── ClassCastException
"            │    ├── ArithmeticException
"            │    ├── ConcurrentModificationException
"            │    └── UnsupportedOperationException
"            │
"            └── Checked Exceptions       " must be handled or declared
"                 ├── IOException
"                 │    ├── FileNotFoundException
"                 │    ├── EOFException
"                 │    └── SocketException
"                 │
"                 ├── SQLException
"                 ├── ClassNotFoundException
"                 ├── InterruptedException
"                 ├── ReflectiveOperationException
"                 │    ├── NoSuchMethodException
"                 │    ├── IllegalAccessException
"                 │    └── InvocationTargetException
"                 └── TimeoutException
"
" =========================================================
" LEGEND
" =========================================================
" Error              -> JVM-level, do not handle
" RuntimeException   -> unchecked (no throws required)
" Checked Exception  -> must be caught or declared
"
" =========================================================
" CORE RULES (EXAM & REAL LIFE)
" =========================================================
" 1. Catch from MOST SPECIFIC to MOST GENERAL
" 2. Do not catch Error
" 3. Prefer IllegalArgumentException for bad arguments
" 4. JVM throws NullPointerException — you usually don't
" 5. Checked exceptions = part of API contract
"
" =========================================================
" END OF CHEAT SHEET
" =========================================================
